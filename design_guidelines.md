# Tify Landing Page - Design Guidelines

## Brand Identity & Visual Style

**Color Palette:**
- Primary: Dark Blue (#0E1230)
- Secondary: Dark Grey (#3D3D4A)
- Accent: Dark Purple (#6B46C1) for CTAs, interactive elements, and chat bubbles
- Reference aesthetic: delos.co.id

**Typography:**
- Headings: Playfair Display (professional serif)
- Body: Inter (modern sans-serif)

**Design Aesthetic:**
- Modern tech aesthetic with clean lines
- Corporate professionalism
- Generous white space for modern, clean feel
- Dark purple to black gradients for hero sections

## Layout & Spacing

**Responsive Breakpoints:**
- Mobile: 320px
- Tablet: 768px
- Desktop: 1024px
- Large Desktop: 1440px

**Touch Targets:**
- Minimum 44px height on mobile for all interactive elements

**Navigation:**
- Desktop: Full horizontal menu with links (Home, Products, Courses, Consultancy, About Us, Blog)
- Mobile: Hamburger menu

## Component Design Patterns

**Interactive Elements:**
- Smooth scroll animations (fade-in, slide-up on scroll)
- Hover effects on all interactive elements
- Card shadows with subtle elevation on hover
- Button states: hover darkens by 10%, active state with slight scale
- Primary CTAs use dark purple (#6B46C1)

**Cards:**
- Service cards with icons, titles, descriptions
- Blog cards with featured image, category tag, title, excerpt, author photo, date, read time
- Course cards with thumbnail, title, description, format badges, price, duration, instructor
- Team member cards with photos, names, titles, bios

## Page-Specific Layouts

**Home Page:**
- Hero section with animated gradient background, headline "Your Digital AI Partner", subheadline, CTA button
- Three service cards section (Products, Courses, Consultancy)
- Latest blog posts section (3 posts grid)
- Testimonials carousel
- Final CTA section with "Ready to transform your business?" and "Book Free Consultation" button

**Products Page:**
- Two product sections: Personal Finance Management and Kids & Parenting Application
- Pricing tables with 3 tiers (FREE, ADVANCED, ENTERPRISE) showing features comparison
- Embedded YouTube demo video player
- CTAs: "Start Free Trial" and "Request Enterprise Demo"

**Courses Page:**
- Filter system (Category and Format dropdowns)
- Sort options (Newest, Most Popular, Price)
- Grid layout (3 columns desktop, 1 mobile) of course cards
- Course detail page: overview, "What you'll learn" bullets, module list, instructor profile with photo and bio, "Interested in this course?" CTA button

**Consultancy Page:**
- Hero with "Expert AI & Finance Consultancy" and scheduling CTA
- Service category cards (AI Strategy, Financial Planning, Corporate Training) with descriptions
- 4-step process timeline (Initial Consultation, Needs Assessment, Custom Proposal, Implementation)
- Large WhatsApp-style "Interested? Call Now" button
- "Contact for Pricing" buttons (NO pricing displayed)

**Blog Page:**
- Category filter tabs (All, Parenting, Financial Literacy, Crypto, Macro Economics)
- 3-column grid (1 on mobile) of blog cards
- Blog post page: full-width hero image, title/author/date/read time, formatted content, social share buttons, related posts (3), Disqus comments

**About Us Page:**
- Company story section (mission, vision, values)
- Team member grid with photos
- "Why Choose Tify" section with 3-4 differentiators
- Achievement statistics display
